# ♻️ Household Food Waste Reduction — BIA-BLASTA

**Module:** IS6071 Business Analytics for Sustainability | University College Cork  
**Team Project**  
**Year:** 2025–2026

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project tackles the growing challenge of **household food waste in Ireland** through a sustainability analytics lens. Using the design thinking framework and data-driven analysis, the team identified key drivers of food waste and proposed actionable, evidence-based interventions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyse household food waste patterns across Ireland
- Identify the key behavioural and socioeconomic drivers of food waste
- Propose targeted, data-backed sustainability interventions
- Communicate findings through interactive dashboards

---

## 🔬 Methodology

| Step | Approach |
|---|---|
| Problem Framing | Design Thinking Framework |
| Data Analysis | Quantitative analysis of food waste patterns |
| Visualisation | Power BI and Tableau dashboards |
| Insights | Identified waste drivers by household type, region, and consumption behaviour |
| Output | Sustainability intervention proposals |

---

## 📊 Key Findings

- Identified major food waste contributors across household categories
- Revealed patterns linking purchasing behaviour, meal planning habits, and waste volumes
- Proposed targeted interventions including smart shopping tools and community awareness programmes
